

## *Connected Souls*

Navigate through a series of puzzles while controlling two characters at once, bound by the same keys. Keep both of them safe, for it is the only way to win!

Even though the mechanics have been made, the levels and polishing is **yet to be done**.

*Made using the Godot Engine 4.7*
